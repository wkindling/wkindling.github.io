About me
======
I'm a first-year Ph.D. student at University of Southern California, advised by Prof. [Jernej Barbič](http://barbic.usc.edu/). Before joining USC, I obtained my B.E. degree in Computer Science and Technology from [the mixed class](http://www.cis.umassd.edu/~hxu/alink/jclass/mixedclass.html) in Chu Kochen Honors College, Zhejiang University. I was very fortunate to be advised by Prof. [Jin Huang](http://www.cad.zju.edu.cn/home/hj/index.xml) during my undergraduate studies. I was also very lucky to work with Prof. [Nobuyuki Umetani](http://www.nobuyuki-umetani.com/) when I visited the University of Tokyo. My research interests include physics based simulation and geometry processing. I am fascinated by some simulation algorithms which can capture the physics beauty in a totally virtual world, generate impressive animations, or assist fabrication design. Here is my [CV](https://wkindling.github.io/files/JiahaoWen.pdf)
